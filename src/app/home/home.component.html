What can Tail Wagging Focus offer you?


We at Tail Wagging Focus are specialists in animal photography. Your pet can be as close as a family member to you, and therefore you want to place a picture of your companion with all the other family pictures you have? No problem, we've got your back. Are you in an animal-related business and need professionally taken pictures for your company, farm, or shop? That can be done! We offer our services indoor and outdoor, depending on your preference and wishes. If you would like to book a shoot with us, please contact us via email or call us so we can set up an appointment for a planning session. There we will discuss the options we can offer you and show you some examples so you can get an idea of the shoot. When all the necessary steps are taken, we'll make an appointment for the shoot, and three weeks later, your pictures will be sent to you. Have a look at our portfolio and get inspired!
